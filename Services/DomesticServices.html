Domestic Services

We pride ourselves in making sure we can provide all domestic services you require. Our staff can help with the light household cleaning etc. All staff are trained and made aware the need to clean as the go along when providing a service. When personal care is being provided the area used by the Service user and care worker will be cleaned once finished. However there are other domestic services you may need and we can provide and assist include the following:
  • Cleaning, laundry and ironing
  • Bed-making and changing sheets
  • Food and drink preparation
  • Help with shopping and excursions
  • Making and assisting with telephone calls
  • Feeding and caring for pets
  • Shopping and Errands
  • Medication Collection
  • Payment of Bills
