Message from us to you

At DCL-PRO Services, we understand that emotional and moral support for our clients and their family can be just as important, so it is ingrained in the support we provide to continue to liaise with concerned family and friends and urge our staff to build those relationship as we understand how nerve racking it is having a stranger in your home.

Our vision at DCL-PRO Services is to offer new beginnings and meaningful opportunities to our clients built on innovation, skills and values of compassion, honesty and patience. We are devoted to providing care which focuses on our client's individual needs.

Choosing the right service provider for a family member, loved one or yourself is a big decision; at DCL-PRO Services you are involved in all the decision-making, including the agreement of a personalised care plan to meet the exact needs of the client.
